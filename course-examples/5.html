Experience the Land of 10,000 Lakes!

Lake Superior

Lake Superior is the largest of the Great Lakes of North America. Its deep blue waters and rugged shoreline offer breathtaking views and endless opportunities for adventure. From kayaking along the cliffs to exploring hidden coves, Lake Superior is a must-visit destination.

Read more here.
Boundary Waters Canoe Area

Embark on a journey through the Boundary Waters Canoe Area, a pristine wilderness of lakes and forests. Paddle through serene waters, camp under the stars, and reconnect with nature in one of the most tranquil settings imaginable.

Minneapolis: The City by Nature

Minneapolis seamlessly blends urban sophistication with natural beauty. Explore art galleries, enjoy world-class dining, or take a stroll along the Mississippi River. The city's vibrant culture and friendly atmosphere make it a unique destination.

Discover more here.
Voyageurs National Park

Named after the French-Canadian fur traders, Voyageurs National Park is a water-based park that offers boating, fishing, and wildlife viewing. Experience the untouched landscapes that have remained unchanged for centuries.

Embrace the Culture

Minnesota is rich in cultural heritage, from the indigenous Ojibwe and Dakota peoples to Scandinavian settlers. Experience traditional music, art, and cuisine that reflect the state's diverse history.

Attend local powwows, visit historical sites, or explore the vibrant neighborhoods of the Twin Cities to immerse yourself in the Minnesotan way of life.
