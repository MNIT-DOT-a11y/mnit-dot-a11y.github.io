Step Indicator

A step indicator visually represents progress through a sequence of steps. It should clearly show the current, completed, and upcoming steps to users.

Interactive Example

  1. Step 1
  2. 2
    Step 2
  3. 3
    Step 3

Source Code

<nav aria-label="Progress through steps">
    <ol class="step-indicator">
        <li class="completed">
            <div class="step-circle"><i class="fa-solid fa-check" role="img" aria-label="Completed" aria-hidden="true"></i></div>
            Step 1
        </li>
        <li class="active" aria-current="step">
            <div class="step-circle">2</div>
            Step 2
        </li>
        <li>
            <div class="step-circle">3</div>
            Step 3
        </li>
    </ol>
</nav>

Accessibility Guidance

  • Use a `
    ` with a descriptive `aria-label` to provide context.
  • Use an ordered list <ol> to indicate the sequential nature of the steps.
  • Use `aria-current="step"` on the current step to inform screen readers of the user's progress.
  • Ensure color contrast is sufficient for both text and indicator states.
