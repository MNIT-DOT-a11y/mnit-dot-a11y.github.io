This guide provides instructions for creating a footer component that meets accessibility standards.

        <div><a href="tel:1-800-555-5555">(800) 555-GOVT</a></div>

        <div><a href="mailto:info@agency.gov">info@agency.gov</a></div>

      </address>

    </div>

  </div>

</footer>

Guidance

  • Include only frequently updated social media links and relevant contact information in the footer.
  • Avoid duplicating the navigation structure of the header in the footer unless it's necessary.
  • Ensure footer links are specific and meet user needs at the bottom of the page.
  • Use clear and accessible labels for navigation and contact information.

Accessibility Features

  • The footer is keyboard-accessible and designed to meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards.
  • Social links and contact details are organized for easy navigation and interaction.
  • All interactive components have clear text labels or alternative text for screen readers.

Testing

To ensure the footer component meets accessibility standards, refer to the Footer Component Testing Checklist.
