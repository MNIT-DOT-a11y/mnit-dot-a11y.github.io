<div class="panel" id="panel1">
  <p>This is the content of section 1.</p>
</div>

Accessibility Features

  • Buttons have aria-expanded attributes to indicate their state (expanded or collapsed).
  • Each panel has an aria-controls attribute to associate it with its trigger button.
  • Ensure the panels are focusable and operable using keyboard.
